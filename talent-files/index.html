Hello!

I am a full stack web developer based in San Francisco. My strengths include Javascript, Rails, and front end design with HTML, CSS, and jQuery. I am a curious and motivated learner, and I'm always working on something new. I recently completed a coding bootcamp at General Assembly, where I learned full stack web development, worked in teams with talented developers, and designed and built some awesome apps from scratch with Ruby, Javascript, and many other development frameworks and libraries.

When I'm not at a computer, I like to bike and run around the city, drink lots of coffee, read a good book, and play board games (90% of the time it's Settlers of Catan).
